Reflection

To be very honest, I had never imagined in my life that there were many techniques and rules around the composition of a picture and upon learning them, I realised how grateful I am to gain such a fruitful insight and utilize it in practice. Ever since I became aware of it, I wouldnt miss a rule of thirds when I see one, in movies, in videos or in pictures!

Although, it is not a mandate to follow these rules of compositions, My eyes would always appreciate these compositions that bring out the beauty in the picture that is clicked.

Becoming aware of the technical aspects of controlling the light and its parameters to capture pictures is another lesson that I am grateful for!

I also learnt the importance of post processing workflow, how necessary the processing of the images are.
